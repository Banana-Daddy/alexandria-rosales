Hello

A storyteller who learned to throw a great party.

I started in the College of the Desert newsroom — Editor-in-Chief of The Chaparral and station manager at KCOD, where a sports segment I produced earned a national broadcasting award nomination. After a summer reporting at The Desert Sun, I traded the byline for the production calendar.

Since then I’ve marketed the arts at Coachella Valley Repertory, then the 11,000-seat Acrisure Arena, and now events & marketing at Palm Springs Life. I bring a reporter’s eye to every campaign: find the story, then put it where people are.
