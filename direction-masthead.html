The Lede Section 01

A storyteller by training,
a producer by instinct.

Alexandria Rosales builds the kind of marketing people remember. She came up in the College of the Desert newsroom — Editor-in-Chief of The Chaparral and station manager at KCOD, where a sports segment she produced earned a national broadcasting award nomination. A summer in The Desert Sun newsroom sharpened the reporting.

The years since moved her from the page to the production calendar: marketing the arts at Coachella Valley Repertory, then the 11,000-seat Acrisure Arena, and now events & marketing at Palm Springs Life. The through-line never changed — she knows how to make people pay attention.

“Good marketing is just good journalism — find the story, then put it where people are.”
